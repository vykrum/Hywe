HYWE – Hygrid Woven Ensemble

HYWE (Hygrid Woven Ensemble) is a design-driven computational approach to spatial planning. It introduces the concept of the Hygrid—a hybrid orthogonal-hexagonal grid system—enabling designers to compose unconventional spatial topologies through structured, procedural definitions.

Written in F# and compiled to WebAssembly, Hywe challenges conventional design tools by treating diagrams as active generators of spatial logic.
